Black Crowns SMS Win-Back System
Built for a Calgary loc salon. Pulled 485 clients from Acuity Scheduling API, segmented by recency (0-56 days active, 57-182 days due nudge, 183+ days win-back), suppressed 22 future bookings, and queued 391 personalized SMS sends via Twilio. Estimated $5,500-$8,200 revenue recovery per blast. CASL-compliant with STOP line and segment cost optimization to avoid UCS-2 encoding.
